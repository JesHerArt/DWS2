Experience visiting fantastical winter hot springs in Nyuto Onsen!

Nyuto Onsen-kyo in Akita Prefecture is a hot spring village that enamors those who visit. You will reach this mysterious and secluded region in about 4 hours from Tokyo Station or about 2 hours from Akita Airport. 7 hot spring inns with a wide variety of hot springs and meals await you.

Japanese “Kendama” is cool now!

A “Kendama” is a traditional Japanese toy for children that has always enjoyed popularity. It became popular as a street sport in the United States several years ago, and it has spread all over the world. It is now catching on among young people in Japan in the form of reverse import.
